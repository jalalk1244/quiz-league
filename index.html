Info

Football is the most popular sport and “the beautiful game” in the world. How much football knowledge do you have? Are you a football expert or merely someone who kicks a ball around occasionally in the park? Nothing is more exciting than a football-related quiz test, though! So let's play Quiz league.

Rules

Quiz league is a quiz about football with 3 different difficulty levels. You receive 10 points for each correct answer on each level, and you have 30 seconds to answer a question. There are four possible responses for each question. When the correct response is selected, it turns green, while all other options turn red. The incorrect response will turn that option red when it is clicked.
